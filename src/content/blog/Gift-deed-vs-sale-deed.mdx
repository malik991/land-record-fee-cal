## Gift Deed vs Sale Deed: Which Is Better for Land Transfer in Pakistan?

Land in Pakistan can be transferred either through a **Gift Deed (Hiba)** or a **Sale Deed (Registry)**. While gift deeds often save on taxes, sale deeds are stronger in proving the buyer’s financial consideration and ownership. This article compares both methods to help you decide the better option for your situation.

## ✳️ What Is a Gift Deed?

A **Gift Deed (Hiba)** is used to transfer land without receiving any money in return. It is commonly used among family members (father to son, brother to sister, etc.).

- No payment or sale is involved
- Typically used for **family transfers**
- Requires registration and witnesses
- Lower government fee and tax

## ✳️ What Is a Sale Deed?

A **Sale Deed** is a legal agreement between a seller and buyer where the property is transferred in exchange for a payment. This is the most common method for transferring land through registry.

- Involves money/consideration
- Used in **buying/selling transactions**
- Registered in the **Sub-Registrar Office**
- Subject to full stamp duty and FBR taxes

## 🧾 Tax Comparison – **_Gift vs Sale_**

| Type of Deed | FBR Tax | Stamp Duty and TMA | Legal Cost & Other Charges |
|--------------|---------|------------|------------|
| Gift Deed    | 0% or very low (within family) | ***Low (1 + 1 = 2%)***| Low |
| Sale Deed    | 1.5–18.5% FBR tax (based on filer status) | ***High (14% and above)*** | Higher |

## ⚖️ Legal Strength – Which One Is Safer?

While gift deeds are valid, they can be challenged in court — especially if not done with proper intent and documentation. Sale deeds are stronger because:

- Financial transaction is involved
- Harder to reverse or dispute
- Shows clear buyer-seller agreement

## 📌 When to Use Gift vs Sale Deed?

| Scenario                    | Recommended Deed |
|-----------------------------|------------------|
| Gift to child/sibling       | **_Gift Deed_**        |
| Buying from another person  | **_Sale Deed_**        |
| Tax saving within family    | **_Gift Deed_**        |
| Legal ownership protection  | **_Sale Deed_**        |
