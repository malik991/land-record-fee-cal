## âœ³ï¸ What Is a Gift Deed?

A **Gift Deed (Hiba)** is used to transfer land without receiving any money in return. It is commonly used among family members (father to son, brother to sister, etc.).

- No payment or sale is involved
- Typically used for **family transfers**
- Requires registration and witnesses
- Lower government fee and tax

## âœ³ï¸ What Is a Sale Deed?

A **Sale Deed** is a legal agreement between a seller and buyer where the property is transferred in exchange for a payment. This is the most common method for transferring land through registry.

- Involves money/consideration
- Used in **buying/selling transactions**
- Registered in the **Sub-Registrar Office**
- Subject to full stamp duty and FBR taxes
