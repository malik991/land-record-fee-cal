<h2 className='font-semibold text-sm md:text-base mb-3'> Understanding BOR, TMA, and FBR Taxes on Land Transactions </h2>

<p>When it comes to land transactions in Pakistan, understanding the various taxes and fees imposed by government authorities is crucial.
When buying, selling, or transferring land in Pakistan, it is important to understand the 
different taxes and fees imposed by government authorities. These charges mainly come from 
three institutions: **BOR (Board of Revenue)**, **TMA (Tehsil Municipal Administration)**, 
and **FBR (Federal Board of Revenue)**. Each of these authorities plays a role in regulating 
and documenting land transactions, and their fees vary depending on the type of transaction 
and taxpayer status.</p>
