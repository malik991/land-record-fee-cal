## PLRA Arazi Moawin Franchise System

Punjab Land Records Authority (PLRA) has launched the **Arazi Muavin Franchise System** to allow citizens of Punjab to obtain a franchise for issuing **Fard** (property ownership document) and **Mutation** (transfer of ownership).  
Through this initiative, individuals can apply online, pay a subscription fee, and after approval, start providing services to the public.

## 📝 Step-by-Step Signup Process
<div>
<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 1 – Online Signup</h3>
<p >👉 Visit the official PLRA website and create an account.</p>

<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 2 – Fill in Required Data</h3>
<p>👉 Provide personal details and upload necessary documents.</p>

<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 3 – Generate Challan (PSID)</h3>
<p>👉 System will generate a PSID for your selected service category.</p>

<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 4 – Pay the Fee</h3>
<p>👉 Deposit the subscription charges via the given bank/payment system.</p>

<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 5 – Submit Application</h3>
<p>👉 Upload payment proof and finalize your application.</p>

<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 6 – Visit Arazi Record Center</h3>
<p>👉 After payment, visit the <strong>Service Center Incharge</strong> of your concerned Tehsil/District.</p>

<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 7 – Office Verification</h3>
<p>👉 The Incharge will visit your proposed office to check equipment (computer, thumb device, webcam, etc.) and documents.</p>

<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 8 – Approval from Headquarters</h3>
<p>👉 Once verified, the Incharge sends approval to PLRA HQ, where the IT team generates and activates your <strong>User ID</strong>.</p>

<h3 className='font-semibold'>Step 9 – Start Service</h3>
<p>👉 After activation, you can officially provide <strong>Fard and Mutation services</strong> to the public.</p>
</div>
