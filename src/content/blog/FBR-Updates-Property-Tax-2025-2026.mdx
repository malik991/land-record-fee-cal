## FBR Updates on Property Tax (2025–2026)

In the financial year 2025–26, the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR) has announced significant updates in property taxation laws. The changes mainly affect:

- Clause **236-C** (sale/transfer of property)
- Clause **236-K** (purchase/registration of property)

These updated clauses apply to all individuals involved in land transactions — buyers, sellers, and property agents.

## ✳️ Clause 236-C – Sale or Transfer of Immovable Property

Clause 236-C requires advance income tax to be paid at the time of **selling** property. FBR has now **increased the tax rates** based on the filer status:

- **_Filer_**
- **_Late Filer_**
- **_Non-Filer_**

## ✳️ Clause 236-K – Purchase or Registration of Property

Clause 236-K applies at the **time of purchase or registration** of land. The tax amount is based on the transaction value — especially if it exceeds PKR 50 million or 100 million.
